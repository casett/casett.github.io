Viral Discovery Efforts

Viruses are among the most prevalent entities on our planet, yet we know very little about viral diversity. New bioinformatic methods now enable us to better explore metagenomic data to identify and annotate viral genomes. Over the course of summers 2021 and 2022, I mentored remote-based undergraduate interns who worked on viral discovery projects from metagenomic data. These projects focused on viruses from relatively extreme environments including exploring viral diversity in terrestrial cyanobacteria metagenomes and in Antarctic rock metagenomes.
